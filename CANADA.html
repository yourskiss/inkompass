Discover yourself with INKOMPASS

INKOMPASS is a paid internship program by Rothmans, Benson & Hedges that enables you to discover yourself by working in a creative, dynamic, and culturally diverse environment. When you intern with us, you will be given an opportunity to:

  • Work on real business projects
  • Get mentored by industry experts
  • Transform your CV while partnering with RBH in delivering a smoke-free future

INKOMPASS offers 2 different types of programs

Head office INKOMPASS:

Want to experience the corporate world?
Work with different teams and functions at the head office and gain first-hand experience with a company that is completely transforming itself.  
Be part of functions like Finance, IT, Marketing, Consumer, People and culture, and much more.

Who can apply INKOMPASS Head-office?We are looking for students who are:
  • Fluent in English
  • Eager to learn and open-minded
  • Excellent team players
  • Able to work 2 days a week at the office in Toronto
Field Sales INKOMPASS:

Always had a passion for sales?
We have more than 500 professionals working in the field all around Canada. With this internship, you will be at the center of everything. Build real relationships with retailers, commercial teams, planning and marketing, and have the experience of a lifetime.
Join our territory executive in their mission to develop a Smoke-Free future.
Choose one of the following locations to do your internship on-site.

  • Toronto
  • Montreal
  • Vancouver
  • Calgary
  • Edmonton
  • Winnipeg
Who can apply INKOMPASS Field Sales?
  • We are looking for students who are fluent in English and French for Quebec
  • Eager to learn and open-minded
  • Excellent team players
  • Have access to a car
  • Willing to be on the field 5 days a week

How to apply? Applying for INKOMPASS is a very simple process and takes only a few minutes. All you have to do is fill out the application for the internship program.

  • STEP1

    Application

    Go to the ‘Join us’ button and choose between the roles of Field Sales and Head Office. Once you've made your selection, go through the details of the application that best suits you and then apply.

  • STEP2

    Evaluation

    If you meet the criteria, you’ll be invited for a digital interview which will evaluate your communication, team building and problem-solving skills. Upon completion of the interview, you will move forward to an online assessment to evaluate your analytical skills. Please be informed that our recruitment process incorporates AI technology for efficient candidate evaluation at the virtual interview stage of the process.

  • STEP3

    Virtual On Ground Assessment Day

    If you were successful with step 2 you will get invited to a Virtual On Ground Assessment day, where you will participate in various team activities with RBH experts, and engage with other fellow candidates.

  • STEP4

    Internship Offer

    Selected candidates will receive an internship offer from us.

Application

Why should I apply to become an INKOMPASS intern? How will it benefit me?

INKOMPASS is a unique, paid internship program at Rothmans, Benson & Hedges which enables you to DISCOVER yourself through challenges and cultural diversity. You will be given the opportunity to:

  • Work on real business projects that will help you develop portable skills
  • Build an international network
  • Receive personalized feedback and coaching
  • Be a contributor to the smoke-free future
Who can apply?

We look for candidates who are:

  • Fluent in English
  • University students
  • Curious, adventurous and open-minded
  • Want to make a difference

If this sounds like you, apply now!

How do I apply? What is the process?

The application process is as follows:

  • Complete the online application process on INKOMPASS website. If you meet the criteria, you will be invited to the next step.
  • Complete the digital interview with HireVue. If you meet the criteria, you will be invited to the next step.
  • Take an online assessment test. If you meet the criteria, you will be invited to the next step.
  • Participate in an Virtual/on-ground assessment (VOGA). If you meet the criteria, you will be invited to be an INKOMPASS intern!
Will I be notified about my application progress? If yes, how and when will I be notified?

Yes. You will receive information about your application progress during each step of the process on your registered email. You will receive an email confirmation regarding your status, whether or not you meet the criteria to move ahead in the application process. If you do not hear back from us within 2-3 weeks of completing an application step, please contact us at contact@inkompass.global.

Can I still apply once the application deadline has passed?

No. Once the application deadline has passed, it is not possible to apply. This is to ensure that all candidates go through the same process.

If I’m not selected for the internship in one country, can I apply again if the program is active in some other country?

Yes, you can apply again if the program is active in some other country. However, please note that INKOMPASS follows the same selection process globally.

If INKOMPASS is not yet available in my country, can I apply to an internship in another country?

Each country has different requirements for INKOMPASS interns in terms of country of residence, current year of study, timing of the program, etc. We encourage you to contact us at contact@inkompass.global if you want country specific information.

If I am accepted for the internship in a country that I don't live in, will my accommodation, airfare, etc. be paid for?

No. You are responsible for covering any expenses that may arise in order for you to be present and available in the country for the duration of your internship.

If, on the other hand, we send you to another country for business purposes, then, all related expenses will be covered.

I am not eligible to apply for an internship. Are there any other positions available?

We encourage you to visit www.pmi.com/careers to explore available positions.

I am facing issues submitting my application on the website. Who can I contact?

For any problems, doubts, or comments, feel free to email us at contact@inkompass.global.

If I don't receive a confirmation email after submitting my application, what should I do?

If you do not hear back from us within 2-3 weeks of completing an application step, please contact us at contact@inkompass.global.

I inputted some wrong information while completing the application form. Can I still review and edit it at a later stage? Can it be edited even after the application period closes?

Yes, you can review and edit your information on your profile page at any point in time during the application period. We recommend that you correct any errors in your application as soon as possible, in order to have a smooth process.

Selection

What sorts of topics will be covered in the online tests?

Topics in online interview and test assess your personal growth mindset and communication, customer focus, problem solving skills.

What is the HireVue video interview?

A HireVue video interview is a digital interview that allows you to introduce yourself and your capabilities to us. HireVue enables you to complete your interview in your own time from the comfort of your own space. During the interview, we will be asking you a few questions on the competencies that you will have to demonstrate to become a successful intern at Rothmans, Benson & Hedges.

What is the support system in case of connectivity or other technical issues with the Hire Vue application?

In case of any technical issues with the application or connectivity please address your questions here: HireVue (zendesk.com)

The help desk will answer your questions.

Can I record the interview more than once?

If you are not satisfied with your answer, you have the option to re-record it. If you choose that option, remember your second recording will be final and you will have to submit it.

How does the HireVue technology works?

Once your interview is completed, the system will translate your recording into text by analysing your response. We do not consider other factors such as facial features but the scoring is solely done on things you say.

Is the INKOMPASS internship paid? What contractual conditions will I be under as an Intern?

Yes, the INKOMPASS internship offers a very competitive salary.

Can I retake the online tests?

No. However, this can be examined on a case-by-case basis (i.e. if you faced technical difficulties while taking the online test, if you were unable to complete the tests within the alotted time due to extenuating circumstances, etc.).

What is the on-ground assessment?

"The on-ground assessment is the final step in the INKOMPASS selection process. It is a one-day event where candidates can showcase their skills in business cases and team activities, network with other applicants, and meet current RBH employees. This occurs in a fun and cool setting so that you feel at ease and can deliver your best.

While this is an on-ground event, currently it takes place virtually as most markets are still working from home. The Virtual on-ground assessment or VOGA is as fun and engaging and gives the candidates the same opportunities to showcase their skills. "

Do I need to prepare for the Virtual/on-ground assessment? If yes, how?

No, there is no need to prepare for the Virtual/on-ground assessment. Just make sure you arrive on time with full energy and a good attitude.

If I fail to attend the on-ground assessment, will I be allowed to appear on other dates as a special case?

This will depend on the recruitment dates and timings of the particular country to which you are applying. To know more, you can contact your local HR team by replying to the email that you received as an invitation for the assessment day.

Project Work

Is the INKOMPASS internship program paid?

Yes, the INKOMPASS internship offers a very competitive salary. Salaries vary on a country-to-country basis.

When will the internship start and how long will it last? What are the normal working hours?

The timing and length of the internship as well as working hours vary by country. We encourage you contact us at contact@inkompass.global for country specific information.

In what areas / departments can I expect to work in as an INKOMPASS intern?

This will vary on a country-to-country basis. However, in general, projects are available in the following departments: Marketing, Sales, Finance, HR, Corporate Affairs, Operations, Information Systems (IS). You can visit the website to learn more.

Can I choose which department I would like to work in? Are my preferences taken into consideration?

During the selection process, we will try our best to match you to a department based on your interests and your strengths.

Will I be assigned a group project or an individual project?

Depending on the project scope and expected deliverables, projects may require individual or group work. In any case, you can be sure that all projects will be meaningful, challenging, and will give you the opportunity to showcase and further develop your skills, whether it is in an individual or group setting.

What type of guidance and support can I expect to receive throughout my program, to help develop me professionally?

Every INKOMPASS intern will receive day-to-day guidance and support from their COACH, who has the same function as a supervisor. He/she will introduce you to your project, clarify expectations, guide you on a day-to-day basis, and provide you with feedback.

In addition, you will also receive support from:

  • Your PROJECT SPONSOR, who will ensure you have everything you need in terms of resources and budget to execute your proposals
  • Your BUDDY, who will help you answer general questions and network with different people and departments in the company.
Will I receive a job offer with Rothmans, Benson & Hedges when I complete my internship?

After the completion of your internship, if you meet the required performance standards, and if there is an opening available that is suitable to your profile, you may receive a job offer. However, this is not a given.
