Discover yourself with INKOMPASS

INKOMPASS is a global internship program by Philip Morris International that enables you to discover yourself by working in a creative, dynamic, and culturally diverse environment. When you intern with us, you will be given an opportunity to:

  • Work on real business projects
  • Get mentored by industry experts
  • Transform your CV while partnering with PMI in delivering a smoke-free future
  • Work on real business projects
  • Get mentored by industry experts

Who can apply? We are looking for students who are

  • Fluent in English
  • Eager to learn and open minded
  • Excellent team players

How to apply? Applying for INKOMPASS is a very simple process and takes only a few minutes. All you have to do is fill out the application for the internship program.

  • STEP1

    Application

    Go to the Explore Opportunities page and select your country of internship. Visit your country page and check the internship opportunities available.

    Go through all the details and apply for the opportunity you want.

  • STEP2

    Evaluation

    If you meet the criteria, youâ€™ll be invited for the digital interview which will evaluate your communication, team building, and problem-solving skills.

    Clear the interview to take the online assessment test which will assess your problem solving abilities and analytical skills.

  • STEP3

    Assessment

    Clear the online test and get invited to an on-ground or a virtual assessment which is a day full of exciting events and activities.

    On this day of assessment you get to participate in team activities, one on one interviews with PMI experts, and engage with fellow candidates.

  • STEP4

    Internship Offer

    If you get selected, you will receive an internship offer from us. Join us as an intern and be a part of PMIâ€™s journey of transformation.

    Work on real business projects and learn portable skills. Your performance will be assessed at the end of your internship.
